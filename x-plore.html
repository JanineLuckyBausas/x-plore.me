HOW IT WORKS?

Register your interest and create a profile. Connect with people
with the same interest and establish friendship. Explore culture,
nature and places of interests with your travel buddies. Go on
adventures with your new found friends.

X-Plore.Me offers variety of adventures on every travel destination
that you desire. Using the x-plore.me platform you can connect
with Travel Heroes and co-Adventurer, and explore the different
culture in each travel destination. X-Plore.Me is a free community
based application that explorer can use in searhing for their next
adventure.

WHY X-PLORE.ME?

Our Vision is to establish a friendly secure community for Travel
Heroes and Adventurers around the World while giving
opportunities to the local communities to create livelihood and
promote progress.

At X-plore.Me we engage local communities to promote cultural
exchange and we believe by creating the platform we can build a
strong community of Adventurers and Travellers to able to
connect with the Travel Heroes.

The teamm are adventure enthusiasts that keep on finding new
places to explore and we want to share what we have experienced
in our adventures.
